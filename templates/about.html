Key Parameters We Analyze

Nitrogen (N)

Essential for chlorophyll production and leaf growth. Affects protein content and overall plant vigor.

Optimal: 50-200 ppm

Phosphorus (P)

Critical for root development, energy transfer, flowering, and seed production.

Optimal: 30-100 ppm

Potassium (K)

Regulates water uptake, improves disease resistance, and enhances fruit quality.

Optimal: 100-200 ppm

Temperature

Affects germination, growth rate, flowering, and overall crop development cycle.

Range: 10-40Â°C

Humidity

Influences transpiration rate, disease prevalence, and overall plant health.

Optimal: 40-80%

pH Level

Determines nutrient availability in soil. Different crops thrive in different pH ranges.

Range: 5.0-8.0
